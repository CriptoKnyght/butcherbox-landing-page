What sets ButcherBox apart from the competition?

ButcherBox is on a mission to bring better meat and seafood to every table. This means that all of our meat and seafood is free from antibiotics and added hormones, and comes from partners that we have vetted for sustainability and animal welfare. In fact, we are the only B-Corp certified meat and seafood company that has third-party animal welfare certifications for 100% of our products - beef, poultry, pork, seafood, and more.

How much can I customize my box?

We offer a completely customizable subscription option in two sizes, where you can choose 6 or 12 products. You can change your selections for every order, up to the night before it bills, and members unlock a huge variety of additional products and limited time deals. Plus, we're almost always running a sign-up promotion that lets you pick a free protein to get for a year.

Where does your meat and seafood come from?

We work with small family farms and fisheries, farmer cooperative networks, and large partners across the country, in Iowa, California, Pennsylvania, Kansas, Nebraska, Alaska, and more. We never source from concentrated animal feeding operations (CAFOs). We source our 100% grass-fed and grass-finished beef products from Australia because it has an ideal climate to support healthy grasslands where cows have access to pastured grasses their entire lives.

How does frozen meat delivery work?

Our meat and seafood are frozen to lock in freshness and shipped with the perfect amount of dry ice, calculated by our algorithm for weather and distance, and based on 10 years of delivering frozen meat and seafood to our member's doorsteps. If your box arrives late and you're worried about the freshness of your meat and seafood, please feel free to contact our Customer Support team.

Is there a satisfaction guarantee?

Yes! At ButcherBox, we put our money where our mouth is. We think you'll love our meat and seafood selection, and for that reason, we're backing every order with our 100% Satisfaction Guarantee: If you are unhappy with our service or our products for any reason, reach out to us and we will make it right.
